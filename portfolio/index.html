UI/UX Designer

September 2021

Designed for Nanogiants Hackathon in September 2021 for the UI/UX category.

Tools Used

Figma

What was the prompt?

• User flow is the base for every UI

• Creation of UI takes time and effort

Build / Design a tool that creates a user interface, based on a user flow.

What Flowstate does

Flowstate creates a flexible workspace for users to design process models with an intuitive interface and features to optimize the work flow for users. Flowstate interprets the process models to generate screens automatically, allowing users to spend more time refining their designs versus building their screens.

Key Features and Designs

• Sidebar for commonly used assets, shapes, and information

• Easily continue process flows with quick access button at the end of open elements

• Minimal design to keep focus on the workspace

Key Considerations for Design

• Allow designers to focus on refining designs versus building screens

• Less clicks needed to perform commonly used fuctions

• Keeping the app open-ended for all different use cases

Student Researcher

March 2021 - May 2021

Tools Used

Figma

Google Forms

What was the problem?

How do pet owners navigate the process of helping their pets through health-related issues. The focus of this problem was on the online interactions of pet owners as they diagnose, research, and purchase products that will help alleviate their pets' issues. Saint Louis University students collaborated with Nestlé Purina to provide research and insights on the online consumer experience.

What I did

• Perform user research to find areas of interaction, pain points, and improvements to the user experience

• Generate journey maps in Figma to show customer experiences through the online experience

• Conduct observational studies on users to analyze for insights on consumer behavior and habits

What I learned

• How to lead a team and delegating tasks to efficiently get work finished

• How to utilize Figma to collaboratively work on deliverables for presentation

• How to conduct research to gather data that can be used to find solutions to the problem statement

What to improve

The limitations of our resources and time forced us to work with what we had. For this research we had access to survey data and observational studies that were conducted. In a future case, more data such as user interviews and observational studies with participant's thought process would be helpful in generating more accurate profiles of the users.
